Return Policy

Thank you for choosing RideEase! We want to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your rental experience. Please read our return policy below:

1. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, you must notify us of your intention to return the vehicle within 24 hours of your rental period. The vehicle must be returned in the same condition it was rented.

2. Return Procedure

To return the vehicle, please visit any of our branches. Ensure the vehicle is clean and free of any personal belongings. Our staff will inspect the vehicle and process the return.

3. Refunds

If the vehicle is returned in accordance with our policy, a refund will be issued within 7 business days. Refunds are processed to the original payment method used at the time of booking.

4. Non-Refundable Fees

Service charges, booking fees, and any additional charges incurred during the rental period are non-refundable.

5. Contact Us
